About Me

  • My name is Kayt and I am an artist based in Raleigh, North Carolina. I create embroidery hoop art of many different sizes. My background includes a Bachelor’s Degree in Commercial Photography from Appalachian State University and I am currently enrolled at Wake Technical Community College studying Advertising and Graphic Design. I am currently open for commissions and all of the designs posted on my site are available as well. Contact me for commissions and pricing details.
